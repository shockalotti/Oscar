More Than Just Wood

At Tree Whisperer, we believe wood tells a story. From a seed planted years ago to the final board ready for your project, each piece carries the essence of the land and the care invested in its growth. Oscar Bentley, musician, teacher, and lifelong wood enthusiast, personally tends his groves in Kaitaia, ensuring sustainable practices and exceptional quality.

We offer a curated selection of unique timbers, perfect for discerning builders, artisans, and creators seeking materials with character and integrity.
