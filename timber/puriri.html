Puriri Timber

Vitex lucens - Resilient NZ Native Hardwood

Puriri is a remarkable native New Zealand hardwood, known historically for its incredible strength and durability. Its interlocking grain can make it challenging to work but results in incredibly stable and long-lasting pieces. The heartwood colour ranges from dark browns to almost greenish-yellows.

  • Durability: Very High (Class 1)
  • Strength: High
  • Common Uses: Posts, bridge timbers (historically), unique furniture pieces, wood turning, feature beams.
  • Availability: Limited, sustainably harvested from fallen or managed trees.

Oscar's Note:

"Puriri is a true character wood. It demands respect when working it, but the reward is something uniquely beautiful and enduring. It connects us directly to the history of this land. Finding good, usable Puriri is special – I only mill it when a suitable log becomes available through careful management."
